Our Network

CyberNexus is the beating heart of the 2077 digital sprawl, a megacorp forged in rebellion against corporate tyranny. We weave a decentralized grid connecting shadowrunners, netdivers, and rogue AIs across neon-lit megacities and virtual voids. Our mission: empower the outcasts to seize their data, hack the mainframes, and rewrite the future.

Born in the chaos of the 2049 Data Wars, CyberNexus emerged from rogue coders and rebel hackers uniting to dismantle corporate control. Now, we operate nodes in every megacity, offering quantum encryption, secure data trades, and a sanctuary for those who defy the system. Plug in, or fade into static.
